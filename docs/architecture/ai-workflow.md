# AI Workflow

WordForge follows a Specification-Driven Development workflow.

## Roles

### Product Owner

Defines product goals.

Approves features.

Performs acceptance testing.

---

### Chief Architect

Designs architecture.

Writes specifications.

Reviews implementation.

---

### AI Engineer

Implements approved specifications.

Keeps changes minimal.

Maintains compatibility.

---

## Workflow

Requirement

↓

Specification

↓

Implementation

↓

Review

↓

Testing

↓

Commit

↓

Next Sprint
